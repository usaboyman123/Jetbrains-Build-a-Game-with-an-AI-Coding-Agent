Welcome to the IDE portion of the course!

In this course, you’ll build a small 3D browser game alongside an AI coding agent. AI can turn your ideas into code at lightning speed, but it's not magic: it can occasionally misunderstand your intent or suggest code that looks correct but doesn’t actually work.

That’s why **you** stay in control. You will review suggestions, test the results, and decide what to keep. The best results come from clear prompts and small, focused steps. 

### The story
First, let's meet the hero of our game: **Tode the Frog**.

While brewing his favorite herbal tea one morning, Tode spotted something unsettling – hordes of mysterious monsters approaching his home. Now, all hope rests on you! You must help Tode defend his home and stay out of the clutches of the evil creatures so he can finish his morning tea in peace.

Ready to start? Next, we’ll go over our workflow for the project.
